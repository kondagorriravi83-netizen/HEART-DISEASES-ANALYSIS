# Heart Disease Analysis with Tableau

## 📌 Overview
Heart disease remains one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide. Its prevalence continues to rise due to lifestyle changes, poor dietary habits, and lack of physical activity. Despite advances in medicine, prevention and early detection are critical in reducing severe outcomes.  

This project leverages **Tableau** as a powerful data visualization and business intelligence tool to analyze heart disease data. By transforming raw datasets into interactive dashboards, the project aims to highlight key risk factors, uncover hidden trends, and support better decision-making for healthcare providers, policymakers, and individuals.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze large-scale health data related to heart disease (demographics, medical history, lifestyle choices, clinical indicators).
- Build interactive dashboards to visualize patterns and correlations.
- Identify high-risk patient groups and key contributing factors.
- Provide actionable insights for preventive care and awareness.
- Enable storytelling through data to improve public health understanding.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Tableau**: For interactive dashboards and visual analytics.
- **Dataset**: Heart disease patient records (demographics, medical history, lifestyle, clinical indicators).
- **Data Cleaning & Preparation**: Ensuring accuracy and consistency before visualization.

---
